# THREADLAB

[![License: MIT](https://img.shields.io/badge/License-MIT-yellow.svg)](https://opensource.org/licenses/MIT)
[![Status](https://img.shields.io/badge/status-in_development-blue.svg)](https://github.com/)

An Android application designed to help users digitize their closets and discover fresh outfit combinations.

## Overview

THREADLAB helps you get the most out of the clothes you already own. By storing your clothing items in a virtual wardrobe, the app automatically generates stylish outfit ideas. It also provides a fun, interactive drag-and-drop interface for you to manually mix and match pieces, helping you find the perfect look for any occasion.

---

## üöÄ Core Features

### 1. Wardrobe Inventory
Easily digitize your entire closet.
* **Add Items:** Add items by taking new photos or uploading existing images from your gallery.
* **Detailed Metadata:** Tag each item with useful information:
    * **Type:** Shirt, pants, shoes, accessory
    * **Descriptors:** Color, pattern, material
    * **Context:** Occasion (Casual, formal, workout) & Season (Summer, Winter)
    * **Optional:** Brand, price, or purchase date.

### 2. Outfit Generator
Let the app be your personal stylist.
* **Smart Suggestions:** Automatically combines items from your wardrobe to create new, stylish outfits.
* **Filter Your Look:** Generate outfits based on specific needs:
    * Occasion (e.g., "party," "work," "school")
    * Season or current weather
    * Specific color palettes
* **Inspiration Button:** A "Shuffle" feature generates random (but stylish) combinations to break you out of a style rut.

### 3. Mix & Match Interface
A creative sandbox for your style.
* **Drag-and-Drop UI:** Manually try out new combinations by dragging clothing items onto a virtual canvas.
* **Quick Swapping:** Tap an item to see all available alternatives (e.g., tap your shirt to see all other shirts that match the current pants).
* **Interactive Suggestions:** Swipe left/right to quickly reject or accept outfit suggestions from the generator.

### 4. Outfit Planning
Plan your looks ahead of time.
* **Calendar Integration:** Assign outfits to specific dates so you're always prepared.
* **Favorites:** Save your best combinations to a personal "lookbook."
* **Packing Lists:** (Optional) Select a date range or trip to generate a packing list, ensuring you don't forget essential items.

### 5. Style Analytics
Understand your own wardrobe better.
* **Wear Tracking:** Keep a log of how often each item is worn.
* **Discover "Hidden Gems":** The app suggests combinations using your most underused items.
